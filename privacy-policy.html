Privacy Policy for Better Alarm

Last updated: December 4, 2024

Information We Collect

Better Alarm stores all alarm data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All alarm groups, alarm items, and schedule configurations are stored locally using Android's Room database. This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by the Better Alarm app.

Google Play Billing

When you purchase Better Alarm Pro, the transaction is processed by Google Play. We receive only the information necessary to verify your subscription status. Google's privacy policy applies to all billing transactions.

We do not store or have access to your payment information.

Permissions

Better Alarm requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Exact Alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, USE_EXACT_ALARM): To trigger alarms at precise times
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To display alarm notifications and alerts
  • Boot Completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): To reschedule alarms after device restart
  • Wake Lock: To wake the device when alarms trigger
  • Vibrate: To provide vibration feedback for alarms
  • Full Screen Intent: To display full-screen alarm trigger activity

Third-Party Services

Better Alarm uses:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases (Pro subscription)
  • No analytics services: We do not track your usage
  • No advertising networks: We do not display ads
  • No third-party data sharing: Your data stays on your device

Data Security

All data is stored securely on your device using Android's standard security mechanisms. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your alarm data is stored on your device until you:

  • Delete individual alarms or groups within the app
  • Clear the app's data through Android settings
  • Uninstall the app

Children's Privacy

Better Alarm does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general use and does not target children specifically.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page. Your continued use of Better Alarm after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • View all your alarm data within the app
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Export your data (if needed, contact us)

Compliance

Better Alarm complies with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies
  • Android permissions best practices
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
